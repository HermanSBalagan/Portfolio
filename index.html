A Journey Through Design and Innovation

Engineering has always been more than just a field of study for me—it's a passion that drives me to solve problems, build innovative solutions, and push the boundaries of what's possible.

As a fourth-year Mechanical Engineering student at York University’s Lassonde School of Engineering, I have applied this passion through a diverse employment background spanning multiple industries, gaining hands-on experience in engineering design, manufacturing, and project management.

As a proactive student, I have also had the opportunity to take on multiple leadership roles, including serving as the Team Lead of Lassonde Motor Sports, where I’ve contributed to a wide range of engineering projects beyond the standard curriculum. This website is a showcase of the projects, experiences, and lessons that have shaped my journey in engineering.
